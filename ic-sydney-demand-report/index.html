CURRENT STRATEGY

How we're approaching paid search today

Understanding the current approach and where the data tells us there's room to grow.

Active

Non-branded, domestic only

We target non-branded keywords in AU. Budget constraints limit international targeting. We already own branded SERP real estate organically.

Excluded

Low-converting terms paused

Several non-branded terms have been excluded over time due to poor conversion rates. Budget is reallocated to higher-performing terms only.

Opportunity

Increase BOFU budget in peak

Data shows demand spikes 2 - 3x in Mar - Apr and secondary peaks in Jan and Jul. A case exists for increased bottom-of-funnel spend in these windows.

Test

Branded x international

Global branded volume is +35% above AU. Testing branded campaigns to international audiences in peak months could unlock new, high-intent demand. This would require additional budget - it can't come from existing non-branded spend.

Important Context

Paid media for InterContinental properties cannot scale in the same way as a typical performance marketing program.

Unlike most businesses, we do not control the final reservation process. The booking engine and transaction data sit within IHG Global's infrastructure, which means we cannot optimise campaigns against the true end-of-funnel conversion event (completed bookings).

Because of this limitation:
  • We cannot point paid campaign optimisation directly to the final booking confirmation.
  • This restricts our ability to use full-funnel performance signals for bidding and optimisation.
  • As a result, increasing paid media budget does not reliably translate into proportional revenue growth in the way it would for a business that owns its entire conversion funnel.

Why we don't run branded paid

InterContinental Sydney owns the branded SERP. With limited budget, every dollar is better spent on non-branded terms where we compete for visibility. Branded CPC is low ($1.55 - $2.26) but the opportunity cost of diverting budget is too high.

The international branded opportunity

Global branded search is +35% above AU on average, and +55% in March. International travellers searching "intercontinental sydney" are high-intent bookers.

  • Mar: 103,490 global vs 66,540 AU (+55%)
  • Oct: 51,270 global vs 36,550 AU (+40%)
  • Sep: 42,030 global vs 29,580 AU (+42%)
