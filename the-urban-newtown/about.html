Independent, local, proudly Newtown

Newtown earned its reputation the hard way: bohemian, queer, loud, political, creative, stubborn. We're one of the few hotels that actually grew out of the neighbourhood instead of being parachuted into it. We sit on Enmore Rd, a block from the Enmore Theatre, within walking distance of RPA Hospital and the University of Sydney, and ten minutes from the Sydney CBD.

We're pet-friendly. We keep our rates honest. We let you check in earlier and check out later when you book with us directly. And we don't pretend the multinational booking sites are doing you any favours. They're doing themselves favours, and charging us for the privilege.
