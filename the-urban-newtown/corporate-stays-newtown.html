After hours

This is the advantage of staying in Newtown instead of the CBD. King St and Enmore Rd have restaurants, pubs, bars and live music venues open late, seven days a week. Thai, Vietnamese, Italian, Ethiopian, Japanese, burgers, late-night pizza. Your team dinner doesn't need to be at the hotel restaurant (we don't have one, and you're better off for it).

The Enmore Theatre is a block away if there's something on. Newtown's craft beer scene is strong. And if you just want to walk around a neighbourhood that doesn't feel like a financial district after dark, you're in the right place.

Getting to the CBD

By train: Newtown Station is a 5-minute walk. Two stops to Central Station, four to Town Hall. Under 15 minutes door-to-door.

By car/rideshare: 10 minutes via King St and City Rd. On-site parking at the hotel (book direct).

From Sydney Airport: 20 minutes by taxi. T8 line to Newtown Station via Sydenham.
