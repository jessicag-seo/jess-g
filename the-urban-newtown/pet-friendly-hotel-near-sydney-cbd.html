FAQs: Pet-friendly stays

Is The Urban Newtown pet-friendly?

Yes. Dedicated pet-friendly rooms. No bond surcharges, no surprise fees at check-in. Just mention your pet when you book direct.

Is there a pet fee or bond?

No bond and no hidden surcharges. The rooms are set up for it. Just let us know when you book.

Are there parks nearby for walking the dog?

Yes. Camperdown Memorial Rest Park (10-minute walk, off-leash area), Sydney Park in St Peters (short drive, multiple off-leash zones), and Enmore Park around the corner.

What size dogs do you accept?

Get in touch and let us know what you're bringing. We're flexible and we'd rather talk about it than put a blanket weight limit in the fine print.

Can I leave my dog in the room while I'm out?

For short absences, that's fine. For longer stretches, we recommend taking your dog with you. Newtown is a very dog-friendly neighbourhood so they won't be out of place wherever you're headed.
