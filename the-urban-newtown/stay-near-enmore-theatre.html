FAQs: Enmore Theatre stays

How far is The Urban Newtown from the Enmore Theatre?

About a block, roughly 90 seconds on foot along Enmore Rd. You can hear the crowd from the room on a big night.

Can I check in late after a gig at the Enmore Theatre?

Yes. Reception can arrange late arrival, just let us know when you book. Checkout is 11:30 am, so you can sleep the gig off.

Is parking available on show nights?

On-site parking is available but only when you book direct with us. The OTAs can't sell it. Street parking around Enmore Rd is scarce on event nights, so pre-book.

Do you have pet-friendly rooms for travelling acts?

Yes. We have dedicated pet-friendly rooms, popular with touring musos and anyone travelling with a four-legged tour manager.
