Getting to RPA from the hotel

On foot: Head north-east up Enmore Rd towards King St, turn right onto King St, continue through Newtown into Camperdown. RPA's main entrance is on Missenden Rd. About 15 minutes, mostly flat.

By bus: The 422 runs from King St, Newtown to Missenden Rd in about 4 minutes. Stops within walking distance of both the hotel and the hospital.

By car: 5 minutes via King St and Carillon Ave. On-site parking at the hotel (book direct) means you don't need to fight for a spot at the hospital.

FAQs: RPA Hospital stays

How far is The Urban Newtown from RPA Hospital?
