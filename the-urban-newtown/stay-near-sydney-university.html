Getting to campus from the hotel

On foot: Head north-east up King St towards Broadway. The main Camperdown campus entrance is on City Rd. About 12 minutes, 700 metres.

By bus: Multiple services run along King St towards Broadway and the university. Under 5 minutes.

By car: 3 minutes via King St and City Rd. Campus parking is limited. On-site parking at the hotel (book direct) solves that.
